CPE

Customer premises equipment (CPE) is service provider equipment that is located on the customer's premises (physical location) rather than on the provider's premises or in between. Routers (wired and wireless), switches, Access points (indoor & outdoor)are examples. Today, almost any end-user equipment can be called customer premise equipment and it can be owned by the customer or by the provider.
