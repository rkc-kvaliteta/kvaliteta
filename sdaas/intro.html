SDaaS

As organization engage in Business Innovations and Transformation, there are no clear pathways to improve a Service or Product, Business Innovation goes through multiple iterations to achieve the desired large-scale Impact and it has most Impact on the customers. This forces organizations to deploy, interface or migrate to Applications which are more “Engagement Centric” (i.e. Digital Experience/ Self Service Portals/Mobile apps) from traditional “Record Centric” applications (i.e. ERP’s).

Under Software Development as a Service (SDaaS) we provide services for full Application life cycle management, provide an Ecosystem Technical Analysts, Application Developers and Expert advice in Underlaying Platforms and Integration. We Build Deep expertise in the tools and process hence your Business teams can focus more on Innovation and End customer experience with the Software and continuous transformation over their business services.

Our SDaaS offering relies on SOA Framework and Agile processes, offered as a Platform with pre integrated software components, which can help you achieve the goals for Smart Process Applications and Robotic Process Automation by collaborative business processes in the front line, and retaining the structured, predefined business processes in back office.

Our application Delivery framework is Cloud optimized, designed to build SaaS services, multi-tenant, configurable, allows customization at mass scale, and have a subscription-based pricing model. The operative word is configurable/customized user facing applications by department/function/partner, not development environments. Our SDaaS service is targeted towards organizations that wants to empower employees with tools to manage CI/CD processes.

With cloud elasticity and horizontal scalability in mind, the Platform is built with Micro services Architecture, Uses BPM/Rules engine, MVC Design Pattern coding and Uses Industry standard Web Service for Interfacing and Encryption of data.
