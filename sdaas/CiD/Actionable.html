Actionable Intelligence

Actionable Intelligence is information that can be followed up on, with the further implication that a strategic plan should be undertaken to make positive use of the information gathered.

Actionable Intelligence goes further than providing data-driven intelligence for your business. Actionable Intelligence allows to leverage its capabilities by embedding its functionality into your website or application. In other words, the same tool that can drive your data-gathering and decision-making process can also be used to better engage, and inform, your customers. Make your data work for you in new ways.
