  • FACE-CAPTURE

The Face Capture Engine is a high speed real-time ICAO/ISO compliant checking solution for static and streamed image content. It offers a full-fledged software toolbox assuring high-quality output for your facial image analysis and enrollment solutions, and the generation of photos suitable for facial recognition.

While processing an image stream, the Face Recognition Engine enables to detect face(s) in the images based on preset parameters (e.g. Minimal/maximal eye distance), evaluate the pertinent characteristics and extract the chosen frames for further processing.
