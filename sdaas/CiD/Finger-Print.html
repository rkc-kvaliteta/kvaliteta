Finger Print

The Fingerprint Capture Engine is used for high-speed 1:1 and 1:N matching utilizing proprietary fingerprint templates. This Fingerprint Recognition Engine is unparalleled in speed, performance and accuracy, offering quick and easy integration in the field.

The fingerprint Recognition Engine matching algorithm can equally perform a high-speed identification search. Identification can be seen as a generalization of verification; the goal of an identification process is to find a person in a database containing multiple identities (1: N search).
