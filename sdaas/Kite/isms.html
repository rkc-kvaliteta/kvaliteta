iSMS

Integrated Student Management System (ISMS) allows any Education Institution (School, College or University) to collect data, streamline the processes and integrated to day-to-day activities, automate & operate from a single unified portal platform across the campuses and departments by providing information and collaboration tools relevant to the users. The portal is both Intranet / Extranet based architecture, users can access within and outside campus through the web.

Deploying isms solution in the education organization will does more than just collecting student data – helps to have a better student information system, admissions, enrolment, academics, alumni services, administration, manage classrooms, buildings and eLearning. You’ll be empowered with the key data you need to create a better experience for your students and deliver more student services more efficiently, which drives both your student's and your education organization’s success.

Key Modules:

  • Student Management
  • Admission Management
  • Academic management
  • Student Self Service
  • Class Scheduling
  • Library management
  • Knowledge Managment
  • Workflow management
  • Catering & other Service management
  • Alumni Management
  • Visitors Management
  • Reports
  • Transport Management
  • Examination & Results
