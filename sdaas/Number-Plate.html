Number Plate

The engine is designed to read all vehicle license plate types at any traffic speed. Security, parking or access control and many other systems can benefit from the fast, exact, automatic identification and recognition capabilities of this ANPR engine.

ANPR reads license plates from many image sources remarkably fast and with the highest recognition accuracy in its class. It offers country-independent recognition in Latin, Arabic, Cyrillic, Chinese, Korean, Thai characters and more, as well as reflective, nonreflective, personalized and special interest plates. It is straightforward to use with any hardware, compatible with any image source (analog/digital/still images/MJPEG video streams) and supports both Windows and Linux platforms.
